Security

Your medical data is 100% secure with Medeo.

Rest assured. Your medical data is 100% secure with Medeo.

Security is our top priority. Medeo is constantly working to provide the highest level of protection for health data. Medeo's video conferencing uses military-grade encryption of all patient data, video and audio conferencing.

Watch the video

Servers & network

  • Our data is always and only stored in Canada. Our datacenters are located in Vancouver and BC’s Interior
  • SSL encryption. All data is encrypted between servers and devices as well as cross server communication
  • Medeo regularly installs security updates and patches on its servers and equipment

Medeo office

  • Sensitive data is encrypted on our servers so that anyone, including Medeo employees, can not access your personal information
  • Medeo's video conferencing uses military-grade encryption of all patient data, video and audio conferencing
  • Medeo employs full time Certified Information Systems Auditors with experience in securing billions of dollars in financial transactions for Canada's banks.

Web & mobile

  • Development adheres to bank-level industry standard coding guidelines
  • Software is developed in-house and are audited before launch
  • Medeo's software undergoes frequent testing for security and quality

Medeo is available for ipad, iphone, android and desktop. We are making healthcare more accessible and connecting people all over the province.
