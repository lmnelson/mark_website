About Medeo

Medeo is Committed to Improving Access to Healthcare

We see a future where people can access healthcare anytime, anywhere. The Medeo Platform is the innovative system that makes this possible.

Through our discussion with physicians and patients, we have discovered a need for improved access, and a strong desire to provide it.

We are excited to help so many people, and this is also a big responsibility. We want to hear from you as to how we can better meet your healthcare needs.

What inspires us?

  • Doctors working with remote and rural communities who want to provide care to their patients.

  • Specialists in urban centres eager to connect with patients and practitioners across the map.

  • The chance to bring greater access to mental health services for those in need.

  • Changing the lives of seniors, those who are dependent on public transportation, those with disabilities, people with chronic illnesses, those with restricted mobility, and parents everywhere.

Leadership

Michael Dingle
President & Chief Executive Officer

Michael is a career technology leader. With over 20 years in business, Michael has been at the intersection of commerce and technology throughout his career. Michael was one of the early innovators of internet technologies applied to business through his leadership and development of Blast Radius, a global digital innovation consultancy. With expertise in professional services and product strategy, Michael leads the go-to-market strategy for Medeo. His responsibilities include all client-facing operations, strategic partnerships, and corporate development.

Michael Smit
Chief Operating Officer

Michael Smit has an extensive career in business, brand, and digital product leadership. Previous roles included leading web, mobile, and interactive TV strategies at Modem Media in London, and launching Academy, a world-class digital content agency. For over 10 years at Blast Radius, Michael led innovations in digital sports strategies for Nike, a long-time client. Most recently, in the role of CEO, Mr. Smit rebuilt Invoke Media’s professional services. He is also an advisor for TEDxVancouver. As COO, Michael is responsible for aligning product and marketing strategies with commercialization potential.

Greg Bell
Chief Technology Officer

Greg is a passionate technologist with over a decade of experience bringing innovative enterprise software products to market for the healthcare, pharmaceutical and financial industries. Previous to joining Medeo, Greg was the founder and CEO of Reverb Software, a successful software consulting company that built custom web-based enterprise software for the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries.
