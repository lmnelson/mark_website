Get started with Medeo

A simple and secure 4 step process

Step 1
Sign up for Medeo

With valid Medical Services Plan coverage you can register to connect with your family doctor. Our sign-up process is quick and secure. When you sign in, we'll send you a text message with a four digit secret code to your cellphone. Search for your family doctor or we'll connect you with one.

Step 2
Review patient requests

They may note symptoms, current medications and allergies. Review their information before you connect with them. Medeo Coordinators will verify that the visit is appropriate for telehealth.

Step 3
Your patient is ready to see you

Your patient is ready to see you. A Medeo Coordinator will ensure that all technical requirements are met and then connect you with the patient.

Step 4
Medeo handles the billing

Medeo powers telehealth services covered for all patients with valid Medical Services Plan coverage. Medeo's software manages consultation history and automates your billing.

Medeo is available for ipad, iphone, android and desktop. We are making healthcare more accessible and connecting people all over the province.
