Prescriptions

Save time. Renew your prescriptions on Medeo

Reviewing your medication with your doctor on Medeo is so fast and easy. If your care plan includes a prescription you can have it sent straight to your pharmacy.

Renew your prescription in 4 easy steps

  1. Select your preferred pharmacy

    When you talk to a Medeo Coordinator for the first time, we’ll record which pharmacy you want your prescriptions sent to.

  2. Talk to your doctor on Medeo

    When you connect with your BC doctor on Medeo, they will create a care plan for you. If it includes a prescription it will be added to your case history.

  3. Your ePrescription is created

    The Medeo Coordinator will send the prescription to your pharmacy electronically.

  4. Ready for pick up

    You will receive notification when your prescription is ready for you so you spend less time waiting around at the pharmacy.
