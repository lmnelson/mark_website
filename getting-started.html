Getting Started

Simple and secure 3-step process

Step 1
Sign up for Medeo

With valid Medical Services Plan coverage you can register to connect with your family doctor. Our sign-up process is quick and secure. When you sign in, we'll send you a text message with a four digit secret code to your cellphone. Search for your family doctor or we'll connect you with one.

Step 2
Describe your symptoms

Before you see the doctor we ask you for your symptoms, medications, allergies and any other important information for your doctor. Attach photos and other files if neccessary. If you have a family doctor, we can forward copies of your visit assessments to them.

Step 3
Connect with your doctor

When you start your visit, a Medeo Coordinator will answer your questions. Then, they will connect you to the doctor.

Medeo is available for ipad, iphone, android and desktop. We are making healthcare more accessible and connecting people all over the province.
